# 🏥 Medical Condition Analytics Dashboard

An interactive 📊 **Tableau dashboard** designed to provide comprehensive, data-driven insights into patient demographics, medical conditions, financial metrics, and healthcare utilization. This tool empowers healthcare administrators and analysts to monitor patient volumes, evaluate clinical outcomes, and optimize operational efficiency.

## 📈 Executive Summary

This analytics dashboard consolidates complex healthcare datasets into digestible, high-level metrics and visual deep-dives. It tracks nearly **50,000 patients** 👥 and over **40,000 doctors** 🥼, breaking down care dynamics by demographic profiles, medical conditions, admission pathways, and financial coverage.

---

## 🔑 Key Features & Visualizations

### 1. 🎛️ High-Level KPIs & Global Filters
*   **Scale Metrics:** Instant visibility into overall network scale (Total Patients: ~49,992 👥 | Total Doctors: ~40,341 🥼).
*   **Quick Filters:** Interactive global filters for **Test Results** 🧪 (*Abnormal, Inconclusive, Normal*), **Gender** ⚧️, and **Medical Condition** 📋 to slice data dynamically across the entire workbook.

### 2. 🩺 Clinical & Demographic Insights
*   **🩸 Blood Type Distribution:** A breakdown across all major blood types ($A\pm$, $B\pm$, $AB\pm$, $O\pm$) to track inventory or specific patient sub-populations.
*   **🎂 Medical Condition vs. Age Profile:** A detailed look at the average age across prominent chronic conditions and diagnoses, including *Arthritis, Asthma, Cancer, Diabetes, Hypertension, and Obesity*.

### 3. 🏢 Operational Utilization
*   **🛏️ Admission Type by Gender:** Monitors the **Average Length of Stay (LOS)** segmented by admission priority (*Elective, Emergency, Urgent*) and gender to assess bed turnover rates and patient acuity.

### 4. 💰 Financial Index Analytics
*   **💵 Billing Amount by Insurance Provider:** Stacked tracking of total billing metrics across top healthcare payers (*Cigna, Medicare, Blue Cross, UnitedHealthcare, Aetna*) to analyze financial aid and commercial revenue distribution.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Source Data

*   **🖥️ Visualization Tool:** Tableau Cloud / Tableau Public
*   **📂 Dataset:** `healthcare_dataset.csv` (Simulated/Structured Healthcare Records)
*   **📊 Key Fields Profiled:** Patient Demographics, Diagnoses, Admission Types, Insurance Payers, Total Billing Amounts, Lab Test Results.

---

## 🚀 How to Use the Dashboard

1. **🔍 Filter Contextually:** Use the top panel to select a specific `Medical Condition` (e.g., *Diabetes*). Watch how the average age, lengths of stay, and billing amounts adjust automatically.
2. **⚠️ Track Discrepancies:** Filter by `Abnormal` test results to pinpoint high-risk cohorts and cross-reference them against their primary insurance providers.
3. **📈 Optimize Bed Management:** Hover over the *Admission Type* matrix to see which patient routes contribute to the highest average length of stay.
